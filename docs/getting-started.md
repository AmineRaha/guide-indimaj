# Getting Started

Get set up with [SOFTWARE NAME] for the first time.

!!! note "Before you start"
    You'll need your work email address and an invitation from your team admin. If you haven't received one, contact [admin name/email].

## Steps

1. Open your invitation email and click **Accept invitation**.

2. Create your password when prompted.

    !!! tip
        Use a password manager — sessions expire after a period of inactivity.

3. Sign in at [app URL] with your work email and new password.

4. On first login you'll be asked to set your time zone. Pick the one matching your location and click **Save**.

You're in. Your home screen shows [brief description of the home screen].

!!! warning "SSO users"
    If your organisation uses single sign-on, skip steps 2–3 and click **Sign in with [Provider]** instead.
